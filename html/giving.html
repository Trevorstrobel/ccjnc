By supporting Calvary Chapel of Jacksonville with with your monthly tithes or additional offerings you are a partner in what God is doing through the ministries of Calvary Chapel here in Jacksonville and around the world. Your willingness to faithfully participate financially allows this ministry to maintain and expand opportunities for the gospel message to be brought to the lost. There are three ways you can be a partner with us:

You can make a donation via Pay Pal (see donate button above). For your protection we do not accept credit cards.

You can place your donation in the "AGAPE BOX" located in the Sanctuary lobby during one of our weekly services.

You can also mail your donations each month to:
